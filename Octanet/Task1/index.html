Overview

India is home to numerous bird sanctuaries that provide shelter to various bird species. These sanctuaries are spread across different regions and offer a unique experience for bird enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Explore Sanctuaries

Few Of The Bird Sanctuaries

Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary

Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary, also known as Keoladeo Ghana National Park, is a UNESCO World Heritage site located in Rajasthan.

Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary

Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary is a popular bird sanctuary located near Mysore in Karnataka.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary is a famous bird sanctuary located in Haryana, offering a natural habitat for resident and migratory birds.
